🌿 Your Cycle Phases
📋 Phase Protocol Reference
Phase Hormones Fasting Style ⏱️ Nutrition Focus 🥗 Workout Style 🏋️ Fat-Loss Notes 🔥
🌹 Power Phase 1
Menstrual + early follicular		 Estrogen rising slowly, progesterone low 13–15 hr intermittent fast. Gentle intro. Break fast with protein + fat. High protein, complex carbs, iron-rich foods. Warm, nourishing meals. Low–moderate: yoga, walks, light strength. Listen to body. Estrogen rising = good insulin sensitivity. Shorter fasts rebuild tolerance. 🌱
☀️ Manifestation
Late follicular → ovulation		 Estrogen peaks, LH surge, testosterone peaks 15–17 hr fasts. OMAD optional. Peak fat-burning window. ✨ Lean proteins, veggies, healthy fats. Low sugar. High energy meals. Peak intensity: HIIT, heavy lifting, sprints. Your strongest days! Highest fat oxidation. Fasted workouts shine here. Push hard! 🔥
💜 Power Phase 2
Early–mid luteal		 Progesterone rises, estrogen moderate 13–15 hr fasts. Progesterone raises metabolism — don't over-restrict. Add healthy carbs (sweet potato, quinoa). Progesterone needs fuel. Moderate strength, pilates, hiking. Slower recovery needed. Higher BMR = natural fat burning. Honor hunger. 🌙
🍀 Nurture
Late luteal → pre-menstrual		 Progesterone + estrogen falling, cortisol sensitive 12–13 hr only. No extended fasts. Blood sugar stability crucial. Complex carbs, magnesium-rich foods, dark chocolate OK. Comfort foods. Gentle: walking, yin yoga, stretching. Rest is productive. Low fasting reduces cortisol stress. Nourish to prepare for next cycle. 🌿
