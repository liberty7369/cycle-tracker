# 🌸 Cycle & Fat-Loss Tracker

**Made by Liberty Copula — for every female learning to work with her cycle, not against it.**

A free, offline-ready hormone cycle tracker that syncs your fasting, nutrition, workouts, and fat-loss strategy to exactly where you are in your cycle — automatically.

---

## ✨ What it does

- Generates 6–12 months of your personal cycle calendar in one click
- Tells you exactly what to eat, how long to fast, and how hard to train every single day
- Tracks your fertility window and ovulation 🌺
- Includes a daily log for weight, mood, energy, sleep, hunger, and symptoms
- Exports to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook, and Google Sheets
- 100% offline — no app, no subscription, no data collected. Ever.

---

## 📥 How to use it

1. Download `cycle-tracker.html`
2. Double-click it — opens in any browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
3. Enter your Day 1 date and cycle length
4. Hit **Generate My Tracker** 🌺
5. Scroll down to see your dashboard, full calendar, and daily log
